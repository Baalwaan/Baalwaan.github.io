About Me

My name is Burhan Baalwaan and I am a Dutch born Somali. I have always been driven by my curiosity, which has helped me solve problems and has lead me to coding. I enjoy investing my time into education and learning new things. The constant innovation of tech would always allow me to never stop learning. Not only has coding become a passion of mine, but it has also developed my thinking process and creativity. I began my coding journey September 2018 with my first ever ‘Hello World’ alert programme and today I have the confidence to build small full-stack applications. Through my Founders & Coders bootcamp experience I have learnt how tech can provide powerful solutions to real life issues. I want to continue this journey of software craftsmanship in a professional capacity and hopefully shed the positive light of tech on my community.
